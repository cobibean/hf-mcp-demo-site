## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective website clients (small hospitality / local businesses) evaluating the studio's craft. The demo persona is Maple & Main Cafe: locals, families, and travelers looking for breakfast, lunch, and coffee in Portland, ME.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio demo site that wins custom-website work. Success = a visitor thinks "I want my business's site to feel like this" and books the studio. Every spacing, typography, and motion decision is a sales argument.

## Brand Personality

Warm, tactile, neighborly. Diner signage without faux-vintage kitsch. Gentle cinema-tier motion (scroll-scrubbed hero), never spectacle.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI landing pages (cream bg + identical card grids + eyebrow-on-every-section).
- Beige-and-brass "artisan cafe" template defaults.
- Stretched fluid layouts that fall apart above 1440px.

## Design Principles

1. Composition over template: each section is an art-directed scene (menu strip, floor plan, specials board), not a card grid.
2. The material world carries the palette: painted trim green, booth vinyl red, porcelain, coffee, syrup.
3. Premium = controlled at every viewport: compositions must hold from 320px to 2560px.
4. Type is signage: big, condensed, confident, but always readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target. Reduced-motion fallback for the hero scrub (static frame). Body-size text must hit 4.5:1.
